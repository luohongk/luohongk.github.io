My name is Hongkun Luo 🤖 罗宏昆 🤖  

I am a Master's candidate at Luojia Laboratory, SGG & GNSS Research Center, Wuhan University, co-supervised by Prof. Chi Guo and Prof. Weiwei Song. My research focuses on intelligent embodied agents, spanning vision-language interaction, AI-based localization & perception, and lifelong autonomy. I also serve as an intern at Robotics X Lab, Tencent. Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. from China University of Mining and Technology in 2024, advised by Prof. Zengke Li. I serve as a reviewer for RA-L, IROS, and ICRA, and as a Student Editor for SANA.
