Youqi Liao (Martin Liao)  

I am a graduate student at the State Key Laboratory of information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing (LIESMARS), Wuhan University. Before that, I received my B.S. degree from Wuhan University in 2023. My research interests include robotics learning, semantic SLAM and multi-modality location.
