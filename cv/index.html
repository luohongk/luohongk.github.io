About

I am a first year master student at the Wuhan University, jointly trained by the Luojia Laboratory, the School of Geodesy and Geomatics (SGG), and the GNSS Research Center. I am co-supervised by Prof. Chi Guo and Prof. Weiwei Song. I received my bachelor's degree in Surveying and Mapping Engineering from China University of Mining and Technology in June 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Zengke Li.
